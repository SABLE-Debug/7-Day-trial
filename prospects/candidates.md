## Verification checklist — per prospect

Before any Loom is recorded, confirm:

- [ ] Funding round, amount, and date — Crunchbase or original press release
- [ ] Headcount on LinkedIn (filter: current employees)
- [ ] No "Head of Design" or "Design Director" hire in last 12 months
- [ ] Founder's most recent X post is technical, not a launch announcement
- [ ] No public layoffs, no public spat, no recent C-suite departure
- [ ] Website renders and is not behind an enterprise login

*One failed gate cuts the prospect. Move to the next name.*
