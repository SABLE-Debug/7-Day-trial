# LINKEDIN DM — FIRST TOUCH

Three lines. Loom link. Sign-off. That is the format.

LinkedIn rewards short. Every line past three drops the open rate.

---

## TEMPLATE A — VERDICT-FIRST

```
[Founder first name] — I spent six hours auditing [Company]'s brand against your last raise narrative. The verdict is short and the fix is shorter. 8-minute Loom: [LOOM LINK]

If I'm wrong about any of it, reply and tell me which fault I called wrong. I read every reply.

— [First name], MOBIUS
```

## TEMPLATE B — PEER REFERENCE

```
[Founder first name] — I just rebuilt the brand system for [Peer Company] in 14 days. Did the same audit on [Company] this morning. 8-minute Loom with the three faults and the three fixes: [LOOM LINK]

Worst case you keep the audit. Best case you keep the retainer.

— [First name], MOBIUS
```

## TEMPLATE C — STAGE-MISMATCH

```
[Founder first name] — Your product is shipping at a Series B clip. Your brand is reading at seed. I made an 8-minute Loom that names the three specific reasons and what each one costs you in enterprise pipeline: [LOOM LINK]

Either reply works. Reply YES, reply NO, or reply WRONG.

— [First name], MOBIUS
```

---

## RULES

- One DM per prospect. Never double-tap inside 14 days.
- Send between 7am and 10am SAST. That hits SF at the end of the day before, hits NYC at 1am — which means it sits at the top of their inbox on wake.
- Never send on Friday. Mondays and Tuesdays are top.
- Connect first, message after acceptance. Connection requests stay blank — no opener.
- If they accept and do not reply within 7 days, do nothing. Wait two more weeks, then send Template B if A was sent first.
- If they decline the connection, remove from the list. Score it zero. Move on.

## CONNECTION REQUEST

Blank. No note. No opener.

The note kills the accept rate by 30%. Send it blank.

---

## VARIABLE FILL — BEFORE SENDING

For every send, the following must be specific. *Generic kills.*

- `[Founder first name]` — never the full name, never the title
- `[Company]` — exact spelling, exact casing
- `[LOOM LINK]` — custom thumbnail, custom title
- `[Peer Company]` — only use Template B if a real peer exists in the portfolio

## BANNED IN OUTREACH

- "I hope this finds you well"
- "I wanted to reach out"
- "Quick question"
- "Just following up"
- "Touching base"
- Any compliment about a recent post
- Any reference to a mutual connection unless they pre-cleared it

## TRACKING

Log every send in `prospects/scores.csv`:

```
date,handle,template,response,score
```

Response field: `none / accept / reply / book / close / dead`.
