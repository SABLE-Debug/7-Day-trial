# TWITTER / X DM — FIRST TOUCH

Twitter rewards even shorter than LinkedIn. Two lines. Loom link. First name.

The platform is built for asynchronous shouting — match the medium.

---

## TEMPLATE A — VERDICT-FIRST

```
[Founder first name] — I audited [Company]'s brand against your last raise. Three faults, three fixes, 8 minutes. [LOOM LINK]

Reply with the one I got wrong. I read every reply.
```

## TEMPLATE B — STAGE-MISMATCH

```
Your product reads Series B. Your brand reads seed. I made a Loom that says why and what it costs. [LOOM LINK]

— [First name], MOBIUS
```

## TEMPLATE C — POST-REPLY

Only send this after they reply to something of yours publicly. Pure leverage move.

```
Saw your reply on [thread topic]. I made a Loom on [Company] last week — same thesis, applied to your brand. 8 min: [LOOM LINK]
```

---

## RULES

- Send during US business hours, not SAST. Twitter is a US-first platform for SaaS founders.
- Follow first. Wait three days. Then DM. Following reduces the spam flag.
- One DM per prospect. Twitter is brutal about double-tap.
- Never DM during a public spat or a launch week. *Both contexts kill reply rate.*
- If DMs are closed, reply once to a non-personal post with the Loom link and a single sentence. Never reply to a personal post.

## SUBSCRIPTION TRICK

If the founder is X Premium, paying $8/mo for your own Premium opens longer DMs and skips the spam folder. Worth it for outbound months.

## PROFILE PREP

Before sending any X DM, the MOBIUS profile must read:

- Display name: `[First name] — MOBIUS`
- Handle: clean, no numbers
- Bio: one line. "Brand & AI operating system for AI-native startups."
- Header: void background, white mark, no other elements
- Pinned tweet: the latest audit Loom that was made public

A weak profile kills the open-the-Loom rate. Fix the profile before the first send.

## BANNED

- Emojis in DM body
- "Hey" as the opener
- "Saw your work" without naming the specific work
- Multi-paragraph DMs
- Links to anything except the Loom
