# AUDIT LOOM — 5-BEAT TEMPLATE

The audit Loom is the single hottest first-touch artifact MOBIUS ships. It must run six hours of research per Loom, then deliver eight minutes of verdict.

This is the script. *No one watches a generic teardown twice.*

---

## RUNTIME

7–9 minutes. Hard ceiling at 10. A Loom that runs past ten gets closed.

## RESOLUTION

1920×1080. Camera bubble bottom-left, 240px. Screen recording fills the frame.

## INTRO CARD

Black hold for 1.2 seconds. White Möbius mark fades in for 0.6s. Mono caption underneath:

```
MOBIUS BRAND AUDIT
[COMPANY NAME] — [DATE]
```

Card holds for 2.5 seconds total. Then dissolve to screen.

---

## BEAT 1 — THE OPEN (0:00–0:45)

**On camera, no screen yet.**

Three sentences. That is the entire open.

> "I'm [first name] from MOBIUS. I rebuilt the brand system for an AI-native Series A in the last 14 days, and I just did the same audit on yours. Here is what I found, what is costing you, and what it would take to fix it."

No hello. No throat-clearing. No "great to meet you." The viewer's finger is already on the close button — earn the next thirty seconds.

End the beat by saying the company's name out loud once. Pattern interrupt.

## BEAT 2 — THE VERDICT (0:45–2:00)

**Cut to screen. Their homepage, hero crop.**

State the verdict in one sentence. Then the cost in one sentence. Then the three faults you will prove.

Template:

> "The verdict: your brand reads like a YC batch screenshot, not a Series B. The cost: every enterprise buyer who lands on this page wonders if you are still pre-revenue. The three faults I will prove in the next five minutes are [fault one], [fault two], [fault three]."

The three faults are always: **signal**, **system**, and **surface**.

- **Signal** — what the brand says about the company's stage and ambition
- **System** — how consistent the brand is across product, marketing, and sales
- **Surface** — typography, color, layout, hierarchy

## BEAT 3 — THE TEARDOWN (2:00–6:00)

**Side-by-side: their work on the left, a reference on the right.**

Four minutes. One minute per fault, with a fifteen-second buffer. Use the cursor as a pointer — never narrate without showing.

For each fault:

1. Show the artifact. Hero, deck slide, pricing page, app login, whichever.
2. Name the specific problem in mono text overlay. Eight words maximum.
3. Show a reference that solved the same problem at the right tier. Linear, Vercel, Cursor, Arc, Ramp — anything that punches at Series B+.
4. Say the one-line lesson out loud.

Banned during teardown: "I think," "kind of," "a bit," "maybe." The viewer is paying for conviction. Deliver it.

## BEAT 4 — THE FIX (6:00–7:30)

**Back on camera. Screen split: bullet list right, you left.**

Three bullets. No more.

```
1. [What to rebuild first, why, and the cost of waiting]
2. [What to rebuild second, and the leverage it unlocks]
3. [What to leave alone, because it is already good]
```

The third bullet is non-negotiable. Always leave one thing standing. Builds trust faster than any sales line.

End the beat with the bridge:

> "I built the entire MOBIUS playbook to ship exactly this. Fourteen days. One studio. One retainer."

## BEAT 5 — THE CLOSE (7:30–8:30)

**On camera, leaning in, single sentence per breath.**

The close is four sentences. Memorize them.

> "If you want the full system rebuild, I have one slot open this month at $30K setup plus a $10K retainer. If you want to argue with me about any of the three faults, reply to this and tell me which one I got wrong. Either reply works. Either way, I read it the same day."

Hold on the camera for two seconds after the last word. Do not cut early — the silence does the closing.

## OUTRO CARD

White mark on void. Mono CTA underneath:

```
REPLY TO THIS THREAD
MOBIUS.CO / FIRST-NAME@MOBIUS.CO
```

Card holds for 3 seconds. Cut.

---

## SHOT LIST — WHAT TO PREP BEFORE RECORDING

Six hours of research per Loom. *Do not record until every line below is ticked.*

- [ ] Their hero screenshot at 2x retina, cropped to 1920×1080
- [ ] Three additional artifacts: a deck slide, a pricing page, a feature page
- [ ] One screenshot of their last social post (LinkedIn or X)
- [ ] One screenshot of their app login or onboarding step
- [ ] Three reference artifacts at the right tier, named in section above
- [ ] The founder's name, role, and last public statement (use it once)
- [ ] Their last fundraise headline and lead investor
- [ ] The three faults written out, eight words each, copy-paste ready
- [ ] The three fixes written out, full sentences, copy-paste ready
- [ ] The intro card and outro card pre-rendered as PNGs
- [ ] Camera framing tested, audio level checked, no notifications visible

---

## POST-RECORD CHECKLIST

- [ ] Watch the Loom back at 1.5x. If any beat drags, recut.
- [ ] Run the script through `noise-cancel` discipline. Strip every banned word.
- [ ] Custom thumbnail: their logo + the verdict line in mono.
- [ ] Custom title: `MOBIUS audit — [Company]`
- [ ] Send within 24 hours of finishing the dossier. Stale Looms close worse.

---

## FAILURE MODES

- Running long → cut beat 3 first, never beat 5
- Sounding hedged → re-record beat 2 until the verdict lands clean
- No reply within 72 hours → wait, do not double-tap
- Reply argues a fault → respond same day with a deeper artifact, not a defense

The Loom is the offer. The proposal is the paperwork.
